# PRODUCT — Carpenter-Shen Lab Website

> Read by the [impeccable](.agents/skills/impeccable/SKILL.md) skill (`load-context.mjs`) and by [purdue-brand](.agents/skills/purdue-brand/SKILL.md). Edit this file when the lab's identity, audience, or brand constraints change.

## Register

**brand** — this is a public-facing academic lab website. Design IS part of the product. Treat every page as a marketing surface that also has to convey scientific credibility.

## Users

- **Prospective lab members** (postdocs, grad students, undergrads) skimming for fit. They need: research focus, current projects, who works here, how to apply.
- **Collaborators** (other PIs, industry, funders) checking credibility. They need: publications, software, recent activity.
- **Press / curious visitors** landing from a paper or a Purdue news item. They need: a clean "what does this lab do" answer in <15 seconds.
- **Existing lab members** referencing internal links. Lower priority; can tolerate utilitarian UI.

## Product purpose

Convey that the Carpenter-Shen Lab at Purdue is the place to do impactful image-based cell-profiling research, with deep ties to CellProfiler and Cell Painting and a track record of openly released tools. The site is a recruiting and credibility surface, not a knowledge base.

## Brand

**This site lives under the Purdue University brand.** All visual choices must comply with Purdue Marketing & Communications guidelines (June 2025, V06). The full rule set lives in [.agents/skills/purdue-brand/](.agents/skills/purdue-brand/) — load that skill before designing.

Core constraints:

- **Palette**: Boilermaker Gold (`#CFB991`), Black (`#000000`), and the supporting colors Aged (`#8E6F3E`), Rush (`#DAAA00`), Field (`#DDB945`), Dust (`#EBD99F`), Steel (`#555960`), Cool Gray (`#6F727B`), Railway Gray (`#9D9795`), Steam (`#C4BFC0`). No other accents without Marcom approval.
- **Type**: Acumin Pro (heading), United Sans (display), Source Serif Pro (body). The site uses Barlow / Barlow Condensed / Source Serif 4 as free Google-hosted alternates — documented and intentional.
- **Logos**: never modify any Purdue mark. The lab's own `cs-logo.svg` may sit next to (not replace) a Purdue identifier.
- **Diagonals**: 77° (matches Acumin italic).
- **Voice**: Driven, Balanced, Intentional, Innovative, Inclusive, Respected. Foundational message frame: "The Persistent Pursuit of the Next Giant Leap." Use sparingly.

## Tone

Confident but not boastful. Specific over vague. Credit collaborators. No marketing superlatives. No em dashes. Justified body paragraphs.

## Anti-references

What this site should NOT look like:

- Generic academic Wordpress (sidebar, cluttered nav, stock photo of glassware).
- Startup landing page (no "Get started" CTAs, no signup form, no "trusted by" logo wall as the primary social proof).
- Broad Institute Carpenter-Singh site (`_archive/` reference) — that's the previous lab; we are deliberately different visually.

## Strategic principles

1. **Mission first, credentials second.** The hero answers "why does this lab exist" before "who are these people."
2. **One color carries identity.** Boilermaker Gold is the through-line. Don't dilute it with secondary palettes.
3. **Show, don't tell.** Specific tools (CellProfiler, Cell Painting), specific numbers, specific papers. No "world-class" vocabulary.
4. **Quiet motion.** Subtle fades and dot grids only. No parallax tricks, no scroll-jacking, no "wow" animations that fight the science.
5. **Sub-brand subordinate to university.** "Carpenter-Shen Lab" reads smaller and lighter than "Purdue" in any lockup that includes both.
