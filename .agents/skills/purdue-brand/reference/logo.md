# Purdue Logo & Lab Sub-Brand

Source: marcom.purdue.edu/our-brand/logos-usage (public, fetched 2026-05-27). The public page contains governance rules but **does not publish** the specific clear-space, minimum-size, or lockup geometry. Items marked **VERIFY-PDF** must be confirmed against the official PDF.

## Governance (publicly stated, binding)

> "No Purdue mark (including any Purdue co-brand or mention of Purdue or Purdue University) may be created, modified, adapted, transformed, reworked, refashioned or changed in any other manner."

- **Never** modify, recolor, redraw, skew, rotate, outline, or composite the Purdue wordmark or shield.
- **Never** embed the Purdue mark inside another logo lockup without a Marcom-approved co-brand template.
- Use only files obtained from `marcom.purdue.edu/our-brand/logos-usage/`.

## Variants (standard university brand systems)

**VERIFY-PDF.** Typical variants Purdue ships:

- Primary wordmark (full color on white)
- Reverse wordmark (white on dark)
- Mono black / mono gold
- Shield (the "P" mark) — primary, reverse, mono
- Athletics marks (separate system, do not use for academic sites)

## Lab / department sub-brand lockup

**VERIFY-PDF.** Public guidance is absent. Best practice for an academic lab inside Purdue, based on how peer Purdue sites compose their headers:

- Lead with the Purdue wordmark or "Purdue University" set in Barlow Condensed (or licensed Acumin/United Sans) in `--purdue-boilermaker-gold` on dark or `--purdue-black` on light.
- Set the lab name ("Carpenter-Shen Lab") in the same face one weight lighter or one size smaller than the Purdue identifier — the lab is subordinate to the university brand.
- Use a vertical rule or generous space between the two — never visually merge them.
- The lab can have its own decorative mark (the current `cs-logo.svg`) **next to** the Purdue identifier, not replacing it.

## Clear-space, minimum size

**VERIFY-PDF.** Common Purdue conventions (treat as placeholder until confirmed):

- Clear space around any Purdue mark: equal to the cap height of the wordmark on all sides.
- Minimum digital size: 100px wide for the wordmark, 24px tall for the shield.
- Minimum print size: 1 inch wide for the wordmark.

## Acceptable backgrounds

- Gold mark → black, white, or photography with sufficient contrast.
- White (reverse) mark → black, near-black (`#111`), or Boilermaker Gold backgrounds.
- Never on busy photography without a scrim.
