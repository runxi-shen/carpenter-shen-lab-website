Runxi Shen is a Research Assistant Professor at Purdue University and Co-Principal Investigator of the Carpenter-Shen Lab. His research focuses on developing computational methods for extracting meaningful features from cell images and translating them into biological discoveries and new therapeutic insights, spanning representation learning, deep learning, and large-scale morphological profiling.
