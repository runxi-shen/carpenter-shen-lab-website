My interests lie in the interface of computer science, math, image analysis, and biology. I aim to develop tools and workflows to process high throughput imaging datasets, with special interest on applying computational methods on live microscopy data. I am also keen on extracting biological insights from high content microscopy, even when using deep learning tools.
